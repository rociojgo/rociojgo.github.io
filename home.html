Ecol. Rocío Jazmín Guzmán-Ojeda

I am a master's student at the Biological Sciences Program at UNAM. My current research is about the demography and natural history of a vulnerable damselfly population in an urban area. I'm interested in understanding how invertebrate populations and communities are affected by changes in their ecological interactions. I'm also interested in biological inventories and wildlife conservation.
