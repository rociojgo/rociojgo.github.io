M. Sc. Rocío Jazmín Guzmán-Ojeda

I am an ecologist. My main interest is understanding how wildlife populations and communities are affected by disturbances in their ecological interactions, especially freshwater invertebrates, such as dragonflies and damselflies. I am currently a PhD student at the University of Arizona, at the Aquatic Ecology Lab. .
