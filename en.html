M. Sc. Rocío Jazmín Guzmán-Ojeda

I'm an ecologist and Master in Biological Sciences. My research has been focused on wildlife population ecology. I'm interested in understanding how freshwater invertebrate populations and communities are affected by changes in their ecological interactions.
